VRIFY Pricing Page V2 Wireframes
================================

Project Origin
--------------
Created: January 2026
Updated: January 13, 2026

Project Overview
----------------
This wireframe project reimagines the VRIFY pricing page to accommodate VRIFY Predict as a suite with multiple sub-products (DORA, Cousteau, Data Augmentation) while VRIFY Present remains a standalone product.

Primary Goal
------------
Drive "Book a Demo" CTA clicks from qualified prospects evaluating VRIFY's integrated platform.

Product Architecture
--------------------
1. VRIFY Data Uploader (Foundational)
   - Enables importing diverse data types/formats
   - Cleans, standardizes, and unifies data
   - Required first step for both VRIFY Predict and VRIFY Present

2. VRIFY Predict (Suite - 3 products)
   - "Exploration Intelligence for Mineral Discovery"
   - Sub-products: DORA, Cousteau, Data Augmentation

3. VRIFY Present (Standalone)
   - "Multidimensional Communication for Exploration and Mining"
   - Creates 3D Presentations

Wireframe Variants (4 total)
----------------------------

Layout A: Banner + Stacked (2 variants)
Products arranged top-to-bottom with Data Uploader as foundation banner.

1. Variant A1 - Banner Expanded
   Route: /vrify-pricing-v2/banner-expanded
   Modules: Expanded nested cards (all content visible)
   Hypothesis: Banner positions Data Uploader as prereq; expanded cards show full detail.

2. Variant A2 - Banner Accordion
   Route: /vrify-pricing-v2/banner-accordion
   Modules: Collapsible accordion (single-expand)
   Hypothesis: Dense layout with on-demand detail exploration via accordions.

Layout B: Foundation + Side-by-Side (2 variants)
Data Uploader as foundation band, products side-by-side for comparison.

3. Variant B1 - Foundation Expanded
   Route: /vrify-pricing-v2/foundation-expanded
   Modules: Expanded nested cards
   Hypothesis: Two-column enables direct comparison; expanded cards show full detail.

4. Variant B2 - Foundation Accordion
   Route: /vrify-pricing-v2/foundation-accordion
   Modules: Collapsible accordion
   Hypothesis: Compact comparison view with on-demand details via accordions.
