## Overview
{{PROJECT_DESCRIPTION}}

This example demonstrates a complete wireframe project with multiple strategic variants.

## Business Goals
1. **Increase Signups** (Primary) - Drive free trial sign-ups
2. **Build Trust** - Establish credibility with skeptical buyers

## Target Personas
1. **Decision Maker** - Executive looking for quick ROI, values efficiency
2. **Skeptical Buyer** - Technical evaluator, needs proof and security assurance

## Key Performance Indicators
- Conversion rate (free trial sign-ups)
- Time on page
- Scroll depth

## Wireframe Variants

### Variant 1: Conversion First
**Hypothesis:** Leading with a clear CTA and minimal friction will increase conversion rates

**Strategy:**
- Hero with prominent "Get Started Free" CTA above the fold
- Minimal form fields
- Feature highlights focused on quick wins
- Multiple CTAs throughout the page

**Target Persona:** Decision Maker

### Variant 2: Trust Building
**Hypothesis:** Building trust through testimonials and credentials will reduce drop-off

**Strategy:**
- Social proof and trust signals in hero (user count, certifications)
- Testimonials from recognized brands
- Security and compliance badges
- Enterprise feature emphasis
- Low-pressure CTA (risk-free trial)

**Target Persona:** Skeptical Buyer

## Key Sections
- Hero: Value proposition + primary CTA
- Features: Core product capabilities
- Social Proof: Testimonials, metrics, trust signals (varies by variant)
- CTA: Final conversion point

## Success Metrics
- Measure conversion rate difference between variants
- Track time-to-conversion
- Monitor bounce rate by section
